Tilapedia: A Social Media Marketing & Promotion Strategy for Tilapia.

April 17, 2021

Engaging in social media to grow business has become a recent phenomenon. The objective is to inform, inspire, or educate through content-based marketing.

Feed the Future Bangladesh Aquaculture Activity engaged Gorai Films, in a similar social media marketing process to popularize Tilapia and counter negative food safety rumors. Gorai, a Media Consulting firm, run through a combination of media engagement and social marketing that has been proved an effective way to communicate and target the right audience and helped Tilapia farmers’ business to grow.

Gorai Films, in the process, created a social media page ‘Tilapedia’ (www.facebook.com/tilapedia/) in early September 2020 on Facebook to dispel the propaganda against Tilapia, among people from all occupations in the country, and to create awareness among people. Gorai undertook a series of numerous awareness workshops, Online Video Communications (OVCs) and digital advertisements. Gorai engaged Mosarraf Karim, a well-known television actor, to make a TV commercial. A number of short video-like messaging has been aired and posted in the page.

Over the last four months, Tilapedia reached some 67,000 people of different ages from around the country through its digital content. Videos posted in the page have been seen 100k times and the page was read by about 60,000 people; the numbers are just growing bigger!

The page actually started as part of Gorai’s revenue funnel – a meaningful way to make it commercially viable. Gorai opened up an avenue for tilapia farmers, buyers, marketers and input traders that they can use the page to advertise and promote their products. Gorai will take a business opportunity for themselves as well as for private sectors that will build on a business network around Tilapia community (including the farmers). The advertisement facility will become effective by May 2021.

Based on the success made on Facebook page, Tilapedia opened account in two other popular social media platforms- YouTube and Instagram.

Tilapedia, since its inception, within only a couple of months has made major traction and became a winning way to build a strong follower base (followed by about 60,000+ consumers) and increase sales for the fish farmers. The page actively seek and foster relationships through various digital contents, and is expected to find success in the long run through its revenue generating strategies
